Macade Rebrand

My Role

Content Writer, Brand Identity

Software

Adobe Illustrator, Adobe InDesign

Timeframe

1 week

Macade fuses timeless golf heritage with modern style, creating a brand that elevates performance and aesthetics on and off the course. With a long line of tradition behind the sport, we envisioned a company that meets not only the functional needs of golfers but also enhances confidence.

Identity

The rebranding concept for Macade began with a chart highlighting its proposed values, personality, positioning, target market and voice. The rebrand positions Macade as a premium and timeless brand bringing luxury quality to sportswear with key focuses on quality and craftsmanship catered to, Millennials/Gen-z who value both style and functionality.

Brand Story & Vision

Golf may seem straightforward at first glance — get the ball into the hole — but there's much more to it. It requires mastery of course management, adapting to course conditions, and years of perfecting the golf swing. Macade’s new identity reflects this same amount of depth that goes into the game by mirroring the intricate nature of the sport emphasizing the craftsmanship that requires a lot of time to master.

In addition, the branding highlights the use of performance-driven materials establishing Macade’s products with a solid foundation in versatility and functionality. These ideas allow for the company to resonate with golfers who not only find similarities with these principles but their drive to play well, no matter the circumstances.

Asset Creation

Creating the logo started off with a series of sketches, drawing inspiration from different elements related to the game of golf. As seen in the sketches, these allowed me to play around with the designs by adding or removing elements like the flag and ball shading.

The final logo design that helped me communicate the vision behind Macade features the flag underlining the full name. This one stood out to me the most because of the styling of the M and how it seamlessly comes down into the flag. Much like the game of golf, the stylistic approach to the serif “M” helped me display the game’s long-standing grace of tradition and etiquette.

Results

Overall, the rebranding project for Macade represents more than a visual transformation, it’s a reflection of the brands commitment to heritage while connecting with a new generation of golfers who value both performance and aesthetics. The project provided me an amazing learning opportunity to the brand identity process and showcasing how meaningful design decisions can evolve into an experience that resonates with its consumers.
